[[notes/math/linear-algebra/Matrix|Matrices]] can be divided by a scalar like this:

$$
\frac{1}{6} \cdot
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\ 
d & f & e
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{1}{6}a & \frac{1}{6}b & \frac{1}{6}c \\ 
\frac{1}{6}d & \frac{1}{6}f & \frac{1}{6}e
\end{bmatrix}
$$
