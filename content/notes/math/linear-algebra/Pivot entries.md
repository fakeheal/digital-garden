pivot entry - водещ елемент; 

The non-zero element in each row of a [[notes/math/linear-algebra/Matrix|matrix]].

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
4 & 1 & 0 & \bigm| & 17\\
0 & 2 & 5 & \bigm| & 10\\
0 & 0 & -3 & \bigm| & 2
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

Pivots: $4, 2, -3$.
