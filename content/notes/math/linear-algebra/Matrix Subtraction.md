Subtracting [[notes/math/linear-algebra/Matrix|matrices]] is only defined for matrices with the same dimensions.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\ 
c & d 
\end{bmatrix} 
-
\begin{bmatrix}
e & f \\ 
g & h 
\end{bmatrix} 
=
\begin{bmatrix}
a - e & b - f \\ 
c - g & d - h 
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

Subtraction is 
- **NOT commutative** - $A - B \neq B - A$ 
- **NOT associative**  - $(A - B) - C \neq A - (B - C)$.
