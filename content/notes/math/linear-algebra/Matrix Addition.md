Adding two [[notes/math/linear-algebra/Matrix|matrices]] is defined only if both matrices have the same dimensions.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\ 
c & d 
\end{bmatrix} 
+ \begin{bmatrix}
e & f \\ 
g & h 
\end{bmatrix} 
= \begin{bmatrix}
a + e & b + f \\ 
c + g & d + h 
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

Addition is
- **commutative** - $A + B = B + A$ 
- **associative** - $(A + B) + C = A + (B + C)$.
