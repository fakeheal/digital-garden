Multiplying [[notes/math/linear-algebra/Matrix|matrix]] by a scalar can be done like:

$$
3 \cdot
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\ 
d & f & e
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
3a & 3b & 3c \\ 
3d & 3f & 3e
\end{bmatrix}
$$
